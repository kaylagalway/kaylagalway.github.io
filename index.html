My name is Kayla Galway

I live in Brooklyn and in my free time you will find me playing roller derby, riding my bike, searching for the best Khao Soi and attending tech meetups. I am a former professional photojournalist and now I sometimes photograph people giving birth.

I am currently an iOS Developer at Showtime Networks where I work on their iPhone, iPad and tvOS video streaming applications. I have over five applications published in the Apple App Store. Although I learned to develop for iOS in Objective-C, I currently primarily write in Swift.

A Brief History

I studied Photojournalism at SUNY Albany and went on to work at the Berkshire Eagle and the Schenectady Gazette as a photojournalist. When I needed a career change, I moved to Brooklyn and worked as a phone and computer technician at Apple for a couple of years. I started learning Objective-C and got a fellowship with the Tech Talent Pipeline to attend the iOS Development bootcamp at the Flatiron School in 2016. After graduation, I worked at NYC Dev Shop and developed iOS applications for our clients. When that company was acquired, I contracted for a couple of months until I was hired at Showtime.
